üìö Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Space Master safe?

A: The software has undergone rigorous testing, but disk operations inherently carry risks. Please be sure to back up important data before operating.

Q: Which Windows versions are supported?

A: Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit), GPT partition only. Most modern computers use GPT format.

Q: My computer uses MBR partition, can I use it?

A: Sorry, we currently do not support traditional MBR partitions (mainly older computers from before 2010). You can check the partition type in Disk Management.

Q: What's the difference between free and member versions?

A: The free version can use cleanup and expansion features unlimited times. The member version provides AI smart recognition to reduce the risk of accidental deletion and offers safer deletion suggestions.

Q: Will cleanup delete important files?

A: The software only cleans system cache, temporary files, and other junk data. It will display a cleanup list for your confirmation before executing. The member version's AI feature further reduces the risk of accidental deletion.

Q: Will C drive expansion cause data loss?

A: The expansion process uses safe partition adjustment algorithms, but we strongly recommend backing up data first.

Q: How do I get a refund?

A: You can cancel your subscription at any time and receive 80% of the remaining fees based on days used. See Section 7 of the Terms of Service for details.

Q: Can I use it on multiple computers?

A: A single account can log in on multiple devices, but member features can only be used on one device at a time.

A: Yes, absolutely. All payments are processed by Creem, a certified payment platform that acts as our merchant of record. Creem handles all payment processing, fraud prevention, and tax compliance. We never store your credit card information. Creem is PCI-DSS compliant and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data.
